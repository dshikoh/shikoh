Listening as narrative.

Dara Shikoh Channa is a sound engineer, interdisciplinary artist, and researcher working between Karachi and Berlin. His practice moves across film, sound art, music, and cultural work, engaging migration, decolonial narratives, sonic sensitivity, and storytelling shaped by elements of magical realism.

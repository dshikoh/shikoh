Rooms

Sound Art & Music · Spatial listening · Performance / Installation
Rooms / Rooms II is a sound project exploring space as a compositional instrument—where listening becomes architecture, memory becomes texture, and movement reshapes the piece.

Rooms is a series of spatial sound works built from original compositions, ambient structures, and detailed textural listening. The project treats space as an active collaborator: reverberation, distance, breath, and silence are not background—they are form.

Developed alongside my research into sonic sensitivity and migration, Rooms approaches sound as a place you can enter. Rather than illustrating a story directly, the work builds an atmosphere where memory and movement are felt through shifting layers, repetition, and minimal transformation.

Rooms II extends the work into live contexts as an immersive ambient set, adapting the material for performance and site-specific listening.

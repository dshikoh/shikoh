About

Dara Shikoh Channa is a sound engineer, interdisciplinary artist, and researcher working between Karachi and Berlin. His practice spans sound art, music, film, and cultural work, often rooted in listening as a method of storytelling.

His work engages with migration, decolonial narratives, and sonic sensitivity, exploring how memory, place, and voice travel across geographies. Drawing on documentary methods as well as elements of magical realism, he develops projects that move between research, field recording, composition, and moving image.

Alongside artistic work, he is deeply engaged with location sound recording and sound for film, approaching sound not as a technical layer but as a narrative force.

Practice: Sound art & music · Film & visual art · Cultural work & research
Themes: Migration · Decolonial narratives · Sonic sensitivity · Storytelling · Magical realism
Based: Karachi / Berlin
Role: Sound engineer · Artist · Researcher
